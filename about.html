About the Club

Manchester United Football Club was founded in 1878 and is based in Old Trafford, England. The club is known for its rich history, legendary players, and passionate supporters worldwide.

Manchester United has won numerous domestic and international trophies and is famous for its attacking football and strong youth academy.
